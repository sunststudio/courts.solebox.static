The Berlin Archive
of Basketball Courts

curated by

A Solebox initiated visual project to create a selected archive of Berlin basketball courts. Using a detached photographic style, the street courts and hoops of the city will be documented and curated by Solebox and its basketball community. The archive is freely available and is made with the support of the Jordan brand to commemorate the launch of the Air Jordan 1 Low.
